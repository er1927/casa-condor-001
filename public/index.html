We're restoring a small patch of land back to its original ecosystem for the first time in over a century.
We're removing all invasive tree species and facilitating the conditions for native flora to return using various methods, in order to supply the local wildlife with the foods they've evolved to eat, and in turn for their populations to grow strong, to continue dispersing the native seed around, kicking off another iteration of this symbiotic cycle.
If you understand the inifinite powers of a tree to support life, you understand restoring native ecosystems all over the world is the only chance humans have at a future on earth, so in the hopes of inspiring your own work, we're documenting every step of the way here.
