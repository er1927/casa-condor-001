About

Hi! I'm Eli.
Join me as I document my efforts to restore a small patch of land in the subantartic forest back to its original ecosystem. I'm trying different regenerative forestry techniques while dealing with the very fast changing conditions the climate catastrophe brings about, and documenting each step of the way for peoples present and future to reference for help, hope, and companionship.
The world is ending, what better way to spend our remaining time here than reclaiming our role as a keystone species and fostering the symbiotic relationships that keep each landscape alive?
